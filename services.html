Power electronics

Complete product design, prototyping and testing solution for all kind of power electronics products with our expertise.

Power electronics
  • End support for all kinds of power topologies like,
    1. AC to DC converters (Active Front End 1ϕ & 3ϕ),
    2. DC to DC converters (Buck, Boost, Buck/boost, Flyback, Forward, half bridge, Hull bridge, Phase shifted, CLLC. ZVS & Dual active bridge Etc.),
    3. AC to DC converters (Active Front End 1ϕ & 3ϕ),DC to AC inverters (1ϕ & 3ϕ with SPWM and SVM technique).
  • Motor Drives and Control technique for
    1. BLDC
    2. PMDC
    3. Induction motor
    4. PMSM
    5. Stepper motor
    6. Servo motors
  • Magnetics design for low, medium, and High frequencies (Filter inductors, Line chocks, Low frq Isolation transformers Etc,)
  • MTBF and reliability calculation considering all kinds of parameters.
  • Loses estimation based on double pulse test.
  • We have testing facility up to 150kVA source and load for AC and DC.
