    Our best practices
    Click to view in detail


    PRODUCT DEVELOPMENT
    • Analysing the requirement and prepare the Concept design documents.
    • During detail design phase, we will interact with our customer for capturing the inputs.
    • Post Detail design we start work for schematic design and review with customer.
    • After, we will start the layout activities based on products standards.
    PROTOTYPING & ASSEMBLY
    • During Prototyping we will select our vendors and start fabricating PCBs.
    • Meanwhile we will start ordering the components based on generated BOM during detailed design.
    • After, we will assemble all those components also build the encloser for testing.
    TESTING, VERIFICATION & VALIDATION
    • We prepare the complete the test plan based on products functionality.
    • After testing we will do V&V for all products.
    • We also do pilot run before sending to customers.
    PREPARING FOR PRODUCTION
    • We will correct or modify the boards or design based on our testing phase.
    • Before releasing into production, we also do one more time pilot run to verify the functionalities.
